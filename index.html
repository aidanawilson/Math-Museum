About the museum

Ideas deserve rooms.

Most mathematics websites begin with definitions and formulas. This museum begins with a problem, prediction, or experiment. The mathematics is revealed only after the visitor has a reason to want it.

The collection is an ApogeeLab project combining history, mathematics, programming, animation, and interactive storytelling.
