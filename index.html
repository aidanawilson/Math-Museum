About the museum

Ideas deserve rooms.

Most mathematics websites begin with definitions and formulas. This museum begins with a problem, prediction, experiment, or historical mystery. The mathematics is revealed only after the visitor has a reason to want it.

Math Museum is an ApogeeLab project combining history, mathematics, engineering, programming, animation, and interactive storytelling.
